# Certa vez, quando eu tinha seis anos, vi uma bela foto em um livro sobre a floresta tropical chamado Histórias da Vida Real.
# When I was six years old I once saw a beautiful picture in a book about the rainforest called Real Life Stories.
# Lorsque j’avais six ans j’ai vu, une fois, une magnifique image, dans un livre sur la forêt vierge qui s’appelait Histoires vécues.

# It depicted a boa snake swallowing a beast.

# Here is the copy of the drawing.
